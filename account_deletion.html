Account Deletion for Ultra 2048

If you wish to delete your Ultra 2048 account and all associated data, you can do so from within the app:

  1. Open the Ultra 2048 app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Go to Profile
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision

This will permanently remove your account and associated data from our servers.

If you no longer have access to the app and would like us to manually delete your data, please email us at devisettiupendragames@gmail.com with your account email ID.
